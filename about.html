My name is Diana Tran, and I am a person who has a colossal imagination. I spent my life figuring out how to utilize that imagination to realize then I can translate that into visual art. I practice the medium of digital art and the vast possibilities it can bring. I specialize in photo manipulation and photography as my imagination tools; however, I can work with other mediums such as graphic design, video art, and illustration. I often create photo manipulations of surrealism from various photo sources to create a seamless, harmonious photo edit.

Why am I pursuing art? Growing up with autism has held back some harshly, unpleasant obstacles in my life, and felt that I couldn’t amount as a child in an Asian family. Being in an Asian family often entails the expectations of being a doctor or a position in the medical field. There was a time where I had constant clashes with my family about walking the path of art, and it didn’t stop me from going towards it. I may have been the one who has always been lacking in most school subjects; however, I found myself to be very passionate about digital art. Once I started to take my first brushstroke on the canvas on photoshop, I kept intensely kept working daily on whatever technique or style to get a hold of learning. I felt that my long time insecurity of not amount to something was starting to crumble and found myself to love something as much as a person could. Learning and honing to master photomanipulation has saved me from a terrible space in my mind and develop character growth within myself, such as discipline and hard work.

One of my most successful projects is a cyberpunk series I have made which consists of a set of photo manipulations based on cyberpunk. The whole process of this project takes an intense amount of research to utilize each photo to their fullest potential to better improve my vision of my work.
