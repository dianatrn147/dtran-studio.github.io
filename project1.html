Will Power 

Video 

My film is about how the internet and our phones can have the power to take over ourselves to the point of dismissing human needs and attention to details that matter in life. I narrated my story by having the urge to leave my square; however it led to a journey trying to escape the cycle. I try to incoporate visual anxiety by adding blurs and breathing noises. This was a past project in my video class where we had to create a distopian/utopian based film.
