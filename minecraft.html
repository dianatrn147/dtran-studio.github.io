 Minecraft 

Model & Sculpture

Digital Media

This was a project created in Art 74 at SJSU, where we were assigned to create a blocky model of our imagination in a game called Minecraft. I decided to base my model on a camera because I have a passion for photography. The digital model was then transferred to Cura to render and 3D print the model. It was designed as a keychain as it has a hole at the top right corner, which makes it such a cute accessory to attach to anything.
