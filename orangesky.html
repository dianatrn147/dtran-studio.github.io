Quiet Orange Sky

Video 

This was the first assignment from Art 75, where we had to create a 5-second video. I decided to make a peaceful scenery consisted of what goes on in my backyard. The video was filmed when the forest fires occurred and caused the sky to look deeply orange. The filming style I implemented to convey was to use the depth of length to focus on the subject and how it corresponds to the music. Most of the subjects in my film were still objects in my backyard, such as my plants. The concept I wanted to present was to show inner peace.
